KCC INVITES APPLICATIONS AND NOMINATIONS FOR PRESIDENT

KCC is located on a scenic 177-acre campus along the Kankakee River. The Kankakee River Valley is just 45 miles south of Chicago o Interstate 57. The region o ers opportunities and activities for all ages, whether they be cultural, educational, historical or recreational.

The college enjoys a tradition of strong community support and stability in leadership, having only six presidents during its 52-year history. KCC provides high quality transfer and career programs, as well as adult education and literacy programs at multiple sites throughout its 1,600 -square- mile district. The college enrolls more than 6,600 students annually and serves more than 4,000 additional residents through corporate and continuing education programs.
